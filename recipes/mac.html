Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese with a buttered bread crumb topping which is creamy and comforting. Easy to make cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni (8 ounces of uncooked elbow macaroni)
  • Butter (1/4 cup salted)
  • Flour (3 tablespoons)
  • Milk (2 and 1/2 cups) *Add more if needed*
  • Cheese (2 cups of shredded sharp Cheddar Cheese)
  • Seasonings (1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan Cheese)
  • Bread Crumbs (1/2 cup dry bread crumbs)
    • 2 tablespoons salted butter
    • 1 pinch ground paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over the macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
